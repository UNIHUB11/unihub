UNI HUB
UNI HUB is an innovative online shopping platform designed to provide users with a convenient and comprehensive shopping experience across a wide range of categories. Whether you're looking for the latest gadgets in electronics, fresh produce in groceries, trendy fashion items, or stylish furniture for your home, UNI HUB has everything you need.
Features
Multi-category Shopping
* Electronics: Explore a diverse selection of electronic devices, including smartphones, laptops, home appliances, and more.
* Food: Discover a variety of food items, from fresh produce and pantry staples to gourmet delicacies and snacks.
* Fashion: Stay stylish with a range of clothing, footwear, accessories, and fashion essentials for men, women, and kids.
* Groceries: Shop for everyday essentials, household items, cleaning supplies, and personal care products.
* Home Needs: Home improvement items, and organizational solutions to enhance your living space.
* Furniture: You can even find different kind of furniture like sofas, beds
User Registration and Personalization
* User Accounts: Sign up for an account to access personalized features such as order history, Wishlist management, and exclusive offers.
* Order Tracking: Track the status of your orders and get real-time updates on delivery.
* Wishlist Management: Save your favorite items on your Wishlist for easy access and future purchases.
Secure Transactions
* Secure Payment: Enjoy secure and hassle-free transactions with integrated payment gateways and encryption.
* Data Protection: Your privacy and data security are our top priorities, ensuring a safe and trustworthy shopping environment.
Responsive Design
* Cross-device Compatibility: The website is optimized for seamless browsing across desktops, laptops, tablets, and smartphones.
* User-friendly Interface: Intuitive navigation, clear product categories, and interactive elements enhance the user experience.
Technologies Used
* Frontend: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Bootstrap
* Backend: PHP, MySQL
* Additional Tools: Font Awesome icons, Google Analytics
